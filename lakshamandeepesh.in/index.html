Data Science and Business Intelligence

As a seasoned data scientist with over of experience, I have had the opportunity to work on a diverse range of projects across various organizations. My expertise lies in the field of Data Visualization, Data Analytics, Data & Software Architecture, Predictive Modeling & Machine Learning. I bring a strong technical acumen and a deep understanding of statistical programming languages like Python and R, which allows me to deliver actionable insights to drive business growth. I have worked on projects utilizing techniques like Clustering, GeoFencing, Regression, CART Analysis, and more, leveraging tools such as Tableau, Metabase, Jupyter, and AWS to uncover meaningful insights. I am also experienced in working with databases like MySQL, Postgres, AWS Redshift, and Athena. My passion for data science and business intelligence drives me to stay up-to-date with the latest trends and advancements in the field, and I am always excited to collaborate on new projects that challenge me to push the boundaries of what's possible with data.
