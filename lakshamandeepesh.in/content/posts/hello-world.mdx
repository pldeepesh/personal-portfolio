This site started as a portfolio. It is now a publication-first platform focused on one thing: helping teams make better growth decisions through better analytics and experimentation systems.

## What you can expect
Most analytics content online stops at definitions and screenshots. This blog is built differently:

- Each post is decision-oriented
- Frameworks are practical and implementation-ready
- Assumptions and failure modes are explicit
- Internal links map to topic clusters for deeper execution

## Who this is for
I write for product, growth, and analytics leaders who are responsible for outcomes, not just reporting.

If your team asks questions like these, you are in the right place:

- Which experiment should we run next?
- Are we measuring this funnel correctly?
- Is this metric movement real or noise?
- How do we avoid shipping based on weak evidence?

## Editorial principles
1. **Clarity over buzzwords**
2. **Actionability over abstraction**
3. **Systems thinking over one-off hacks**
4. **Durability over trend chasing**

## What comes next
The initial content cluster covers experimentation frameworks, funnel diagnostics, pricing analytics, and attribution under imperfect data conditions.

You will also see a product layer evolve here. I plan to launch small focused tools for experimentation QA and growth guardrails, and this site is the foundation.
